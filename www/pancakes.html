Ingredients:

  • 1 cup self-rising flour
  • 2 tbsp Chelsea White Sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest
  • 1 cup milk or soy rice milk
  • 1 egg lightly beaten
  • 30g butter melted

Instructions:

Sift the flour and Chelsea White Sugar into a bowl. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients.  Add the lemon zest, egg, milk and the melted butter and whisk to combine.

For each pancake, place 2 tablespoons of batter into the pan. Cook for 2 minutes.
